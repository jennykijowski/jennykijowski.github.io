About

Nico and Luca are a couple of 5-year-old twin kids who love to imagine themselves in awesome alternate realities. Thusly, they have ruled that dinnertime will also be storytime, during which we're all challenged with coming up with awesome stories that ideally involve Luca and Nico as central characters doing awesome things. The success of one's story is judged by the inclusion of as many of the following elements as possible:

  • royalty
  • rainbow dragons
  • candy
  • vampires
  • fairies
  • horses
  • gems
  • robots
  • aliens
  • superhuman powers
  • wooly mammoths
  • dinosaurs
  • meteors
  • rocketships
  • magic
  • ad infinitum...
